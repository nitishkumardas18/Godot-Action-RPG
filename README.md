# 🗡️ Mini RPG Game - A Pixel Art Adventure 🌟

Hey there! 👋 Welcome to my **Mini RPG Game**. 

This is a passion project built using **Godot Engine 4**. I wanted to create a fast-paced, incredibly juicy, top-down action RPG that feels amazing to play. What started as a simple hack-and-slash has evolved into a feature-rich, infinite-scaling combat game.

Whether you want to relax by the campfire or get your adrenaline pumping by fighting hordes of Slime Bosses, this game has it all! 🔥

---

## ✨ Features That Make It Special

I spent a lot of time "polishing" the game feel. Here's what you can expect:

*   **⚔️ Epic Combat System:** 
    *   3 Attack Types: **Melee (Sword)**, **Magic Orbs (F)**, and **Piercing Shurikens (E)**.
    *   Combat is extremely satisfying with **Screen Shake**, **Hit Stop (Time Freeze on Crits)**, and **Enemy Knockbacks**.
*   **🔵 Mana & Dash I-Frames:** Strategic combat! Manage your regenerating MP bar to cast spells, and use your Dash (Spacebar) to get temporary invincibility (I-Frames) to dodge incoming attacks!
*   **📈 Infinite Scaling & Waves:** The game gets harder the longer you survive. Enemies level up with you, spawning dangerous variants like the invisible Ghost Slime and the giant Boss Slime.
*   **🐶 Companion Pet:** You're not alone! A little floating pet follows you around and shoots lasers at enemies automatically.
*   **🌧️ Dynamic Weather & Time:** A real-time Day/Night cycle, glowing fireflies at dusk, and dynamic rain storms with particle physics.
*   **🏕️ Exploration & Foraging:** Break bushes to find hidden **Red Berries**, loot Treasure Chests for gold, or just relax next to the **Campfires** to regenerate Health and Mana.
*   **💎 Boss Cores & Economy:** Defeat the massive Boss Slime to get "Boss Cores" for permanent stat upgrades. Trade your collected gold coins at the Traveling Merchant.
*   **🗺️ Interactive Minimap:** Never get lost! A real-time minimap tracks enemies (red dots) and the merchant (blue dot).

## 🎮 How to Play

### Controls
| Action | Keybinding | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Move** | `Arrow Keys` | Move up, down, left, right |
| **Melee Attack** | `Left Click` or `Z` | Standard sword attack |
| **Magic Orb** | `F` | Shoots a glowing orb (Costs 20 Mana) |
| **Shuriken** | `E` | Throws a piercing ninja star (Costs 15 Mana) |
| **Dash / Dodge** | `Spacebar` | Fast dash with Invincibility Frames! |
| **Pause/Menu** | `Esc` | Pause the game to view your stats |

### Pro-Tips for Survival:
1. **Watch your Mana (Blue Bar):** Don't spam your spells. Let your mana regenerate.
2. **Campfires are safe zones:** Stand near a campfire to heal both HP and MP over time.
3. **Dash into danger:** Your dash has I-frames! You can dash *through* enemy attacks without taking damage.
4. **Break the Bushes:** If you're low on health, destroy bushes. They might drop a health potion or some berries.
